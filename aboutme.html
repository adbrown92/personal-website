About Me

I am a quick learner and enjoy developing new skills that i find interesting, such as learning the code languages used in the making of this website.

For most of my professional life i was a Plumber, focused on fault finding and repairing boilers and heating / hot water systems in general.

While i enjoyed the puzzle like aspect of the job, i often thought i belonged within the tech industry, as i have spent most of my life around computers.

After about 14 years of being a Plumber i decided to move on from Plumbing, taking a sort of stop-gap job as an Assembly Operative while i figured out how to gain more tech related skills.

I decided to take a coding and cybersecurity course and hope to use the certifications i gained to move into a tech related role.

Most of my spare time i'm on my computer, either playing games with real life or online friends, or making models to 3D print, or even just learning more coding skills.

I have a wonderful dog that gets me out of the house now and then, mostly taking her to a local field or just round the block.

I like to holiday once a year in Portugal, down in the algarve and am a big swimmer, spending most of my time either in a swimming pool or the sea.
